--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Input Format
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
N (integer) - number of vertices
E (integer) - number of edges
E lines of pair of space separated integers representing start vertex and end
vertex of E edges.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Constraints
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1 <= N <= 10
1 <= E <= NC2
0 <= Vi1, Vi2 <= N - 1
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Output Format
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
P:[Comma and space separated vces forming hamiltonian path]
C:[Comma and space separated vces forming hamiltonian cycle]
... all such paths and cycles in separate lines
