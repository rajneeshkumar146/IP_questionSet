Hackerrank Link
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
https://www.hackerrank.com/contests/pepjava-interviewprep/challenges/pep-javaip-3strings-83anagramsubstringsearch
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Input Format
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. src - String, the source string.
2. pat - String, the pattern to search.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Constraints
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. src has non-zero length.
2. pat has length smaller than src.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Output Format
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Space separated integers, representing starting indices of positions where a
pattern or its anagram occurs in the source string.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Test Case 1 - Sample
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
BACDGABCDA
ABCD
################################################################################
0 5 6
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Test Case 2 - Real
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
AAABABAA
AABA
################################################################################
0 1 4
